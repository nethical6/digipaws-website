About

Our non-profit mission is to create
tools that help you navigate
and improve your life in the modern world.


via GIPHY

Founded in late April 2024, DigiPaws is an open-source, free platform created by Nethical, a 16-year-old developer with a passion for addressing one of today's most pressing and often overlooked issues: "Screen Addiction." DigiPaws is designed to help individuals of all age groups regain control over their digital habits through innovative, research-driven strategies. Our platform combines the power of technology with behavioral science to offer unique Github like versatile blockers, gamified challenges, and personalized modes of operation. These tools are designed to disrupt addictive patterns and promote healthier screen usage. We are committed to continuously evolving our platform, experimenting with new approaches, and engaging with our community to create the most effective solutions to beat screen addiction. At DigiPaws, we believe that by providing accessible and effective tools, we can empower individuals to reclaim their focus, productivity, and overall well-being in an increasingly digital world.
